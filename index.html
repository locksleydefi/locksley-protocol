HOW IT WORKS
No VCs. No pre-mine. No team allocation. Fair launch.
STEP 1
James seeds 3 LPs
$100 CASHCAT-ETH LP. $100 FLETCH-ETH LP (sets FLETCH price). $100 YEW-ETH LP (sets YEW price). Total $300 bootstraps all pools.
STEP 2
Degens stake in GRAZE
Stake CASHCAT-ETH LP in GRAZE vault. Earn 0.5 FLETCH/block (halved 50% every 30 days). FLETCH price seeded at ~£0.039.
STEP 3
FLETCH drives YEW demand
FLETCH isn't just a reward — it's needed to stake in the YEW vault. Holders have reason not to sell. Reduced sell pressure = price support.
STEP 4
GRAZE harvests = YEW buys
Every GRAZE harvest triggers fees. 50% of fees swap for YEW on the open market. Automatic, continuous buy pressure on YEW every single harvest.
STEP 5
Stake FLETCH → Earn YEW
Farming FLETCH-ETH LP and staking it in the YEW vault earns YEW at 0.05/block. YEW's supply is 10× more scarce than FLETCH.
STEP 6
Protocol grows. Fees compound.
25% of fees build protocol-owned LP. Team gets 25% in ETH. Treasury accumulates YEW-ETH LP. Everyone wins as TVL grows.
