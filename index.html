HOW IT WORKS
Two vaults. Continuous buy pressure. No VCs.
STEP 1
Protocol seeds the LP pools
FLETCH-ETH LP and YEW-ETH LP seeded at launch. CASHCAT-ETH LP available on Robinhood DEX. All pools bootstrapped.
STEP 2
Degens stake in GRAZE
Stake CASHCAT-ETH LP in GRAZE vault. Earn 0.5 FLETCH/block (halved 50% every 30 days).
STEP 3
FLETCH drives YEW demand
FLETCH isn't just a reward — it's needed to stake in the YEW vault. Holders have reason not to sell.
STEP 4
GRAZE harvests = YEW buys
Every GRAZE harvest triggers fees. 50% of fees swap for YEW on the open market automatically.
STEP 5
Stake FLETCH → Earn YEW
Farming FLETCH-ETH LP and staking in the YEW vault earns YEW at 0.05/block.
STEP 6
Protocol grows. Fees compound.
25% of fees build protocol-owned LP. Team gets 25% in ETH. Treasury accumulates YEW-ETH LP.
